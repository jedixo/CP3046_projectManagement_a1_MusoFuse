Mission Statement

The purpose of this website is to promote the events and existence of the Townsville Community Music Centre in order to: increase and engage the membership of the TMCM; increase ticket sales; and finally, to encourage the participation and involvement of artists.

Pluses:

  • Although a little difficult to get used to, GitHub was great and easy to use in terms of sharing information and files and monitoring progress of other team members.

Minuses:

  • The fact that the CP1406 had to wait for the .PHP to be updated when it suited the CP2010 students was a hindrance.
  • It took some time for CP1406 students to get used to syncing GitHub.
  • CP1406 students did not know how to update the server, therefore it was left to CP2010 students who believe they have a heavier workload.
  • PHP is a pain to work with when it came to fixing styling problems.

Interesting:

  • It was interesting to see how the .PHP worked with the HTML and how things came together as a database.
